Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is known as the "City of Light." It is famous for its stunning architecture, rich history, and world-class cuisine.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower - The iconic symbol of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum - Home to the Mona Lisa and other famous artworks.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral - A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées - A famous avenue with shopping and entertainment.
  5. Sacré-Cœur Basilica - A stunning basilica with a panoramic city view.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants - Flaky and buttery French pastries.
  • Macarons - Colorful and delicious sweet treats.
  • Ratatouille - A traditional vegetable dish.
  • French Onion Soup - A flavorful and comforting soup.
  • Crème Brûlée - A creamy dessert with a caramelized top.
